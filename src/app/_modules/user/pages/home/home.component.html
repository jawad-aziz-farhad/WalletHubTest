What is a Ng-Conent?

Dynamic content. That is the simplest way to explain what ng-content provides. You use the tag as a placeholder for that dynamic content, then when the template is parsed Angular will replace that placeholder tag with your content. Think of it like curly brace interpolation, but on a bigger scale.
